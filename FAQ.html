Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is talabat?

talabat is a leading online food delivery service that operates in Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar, Jordan, Egypt and Iraq.

We seamlessly connect customers with their favorite restaurants. It takes just few taps from our platform to place an order through talabat from your favorite place.

What does Talabat do?

We simply take your submitted order and send it to the restaurant through a completely automated process, so you don’t have to deal with all the hassle of ordering and we make sure that you receive your order on time, every-time!

Why should I use talabat on a phone?

talabat is the one huge food court for many restaurants, so you don’t need to go through the hassle of finding restaurants’ numbers, waiting on hold, or getting a busy signal while dialing a restaurant number, or getting the wrong order due to miscommunication over the phone! Besides, by using talabat, you can view menus with pictures of all your favorite restaurants on our easy-to-use website and app.

How much will it cost me to use talabat services?

The only extra charges that might be applied are the restaurant’s delivery fees.

Do you have Debit Card/Credit Card services?

Yes, most of the restaurants on talabat support online payment options for Debit Card/Credit Card. Here you can find all online payment methods we provide based on countries:

  • Kuwait: American Express, K-Net, MasterCard, and Visa.
  • KSA: Apple Pay, MasterCard, SADAD, and Visa.
  • UAE: American Express, Apple Pay, MasterCard, Visa, and Visa Checkout.
  • Bahrain: American Express, Benefit (Debit Card), MasterCard, and Visa.
  • Oman: American Express, MasterCard, Oman Net, and Visa.
  • Qatar: American Express, MasterCard, and Visa.
  • Jordan: American Express, MasterCard, and Visa.
  • Egypt: MasterCard and Visa.
  • Iraq: Cash payment only.
Do you have special offers?
Yes. You can view the latest restaurant promotions and discount coupon in offers tab.
How do I place an order on Talabat?
Go to talabat app, log in with your account, then place an order from your favorite restaurant.
Can I make a payment online in a foreign currency?
talabat currently only accepts Egyptian pounds.
Does talabat automatically deduct from my card even if the order isn't completed?
Banks may place a temporary credit hold to your card in the amount of 20 EGP to check that the card is active and working. This hold is a standard banking procedure. It is temporary and the full amount will be released back to your account within 72 hours.
I entered my email/password combination wrong and now my account is blocked, what do I do?

If you entered the wrong combination of email/password repeatedly up to 5 times, your account will be blocked temporarily. The system will automatically email you a verification link where you need to unblock your account. In this email, we will also ask you to choose a new password.

What does the countdown counter on the talabat application represent? I don't understand it.

When you place an order on the Android or iOS application you will autmatically move to a order process tracker screen. The tracker checks the restaurants' progress towards accepting and then completing the order. These status updates are given based on live updates that the restaurants input into our application to show that they have started working on your order and their best estimate for the delivery time.

The countdown counter is provided by the restaurant's staff as a best estimate for the time to complete the delivery and is customized for your order by the restaurant. It may vary from the estimated delivery time given in the restaurant listing screen depending on the restaurant's capacity and traffic.

I paid online and then my order was cancelled. How long will it take for my online payment to be refunded?

In most cases, the online payment was not charged but only put on hold by the bank. the bank immediately releases the payment hold upon canceled orders. Most of the time this should be reflected in your account within 24 hours. Some credit card provider banks, however, can take 7-14 days to release the funds after talabat requests the release of the hold. This is outside of talabat's control and the best course of action, if this happens, is to call your bank.

If I placed an order, how long does it take to receive the order?
It depends on the restaurant's estimated delivery time. Each restaurant will display its order delivery time in the restaurant's "Info" section. However, the time may vary depending on the road traffic congestion.
How can I cancel my order?

The best way to cancel your order is to contact customer care through our live chat, so we can forward your cancelation request to the restaurant.

What do the icons on your website and mobile app mean?

Visa: This restaurant accept online payment
% : This restaurant is currently running an offer or discount

I just placed an order, but I’m not sure if you got it. What do I do?

Once you are logged in to the app, go to the "My Orders" section and see if your order is listed. If your order is listed, then check the order status as “Successful”.

Can I add an item or modify my order after the order has been accepted?

Yes you can. Contact us through our live chat feature and we will be happy to make the modifcations you need even after placing the order.

Can I re-order a previous order?

Yes. In the app, you can simply go to the "My Orders" section and choose an order from your previous orders, and re-order it.
